Some of my skills

User Research

User research is a way to really learn about the people you are trying to serve, It is important to lose all of your pre-existing assumptions and take the time to truly be open to thoughts and ideas that you didn’t even consider.

User Empathy

Naturally empathic person, who uses learned tools to truly understand user motivations. Everyone thinks very differently, so it’s important to lead with an open mind.

Qualitative Interviews

Qualitative interviews are a great chance to really observe a person’s behaviors and motivations. I always enjoy hearing different perspectives and learning something new every time.
